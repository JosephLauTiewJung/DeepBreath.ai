Best Practices for Drafting
Use Standardized Lexicon: Use terms consistent with BI-RADS (Breast), PI-RADS (Prostate), or LI-RADS (Liver) where applicable.

Be Decisive: Avoid "hedging" unless necessary. Use "suggestive of" or "consistent with" rather than vague language.

Highlight Critical Results: If there is a life-threatening finding (e.g., tension pneumothorax), document that the referring physician was notified immediately.
